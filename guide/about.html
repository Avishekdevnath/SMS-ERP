// Our Story

Why We Built This

SMS ERP started as an internal tool for a small bootcamp running cohorts of 20 students. The founders were running admissions in Google Forms, tracking mentor assignments in Notion, and managing mission progress in Airtable. Each system talked to none of the others.

After the third cohort, it became clear: the real bottleneck wasn't teaching ‚Äî it was operations. SREs were spending 40% of their time chasing data across tools. Managers had no visibility. Students had no single place to track their progress.

We built SMS ERP to collapse that operational overhead into a single, role-aware system that gives everyone exactly what they need ‚Äî nothing more, nothing less. It's opinionated enough to work out of the box, and extensible enough to grow with your institution.

Today SMS ERP serves EdTech startups, coaching institutes, corporate training programs, and bootcamps across the world. The codebase is MIT-licensed and open source ‚Äî because good infrastructure should be free.
